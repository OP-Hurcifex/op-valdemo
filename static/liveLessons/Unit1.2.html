Present Simple & Present Continuous va to be

Present Simple (Oddiy hozirgi zamon)

Present Simple — odatdagi, muntazam yoki umumiy haqiqatlarni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi.

  • Foydalanish: odatkorlik (every day, usually), umumiy haqiqatlar, jadval va rasmiy so‘zlashuvlar.
  • Tuziq: Subject + base verb (3-shaxs birlikda -s qo‘shiladi).
  • Misollar:
    She works at seven. — U ertalab soat yettida ishlaydi.
    They live in Tashkent. — Ular Toshkentda yashaydilar.
  • Salbiy shakl: subject + do/does + not + base verb.
    He does not (doesn’t) like tea. — U choyni yoqtirmaydi.
  • So‘roq shakl: Do/Does + subject + base verb?
    Do you speak English? — Siz ingliz tilida gapirasizmi?

Signal so‘zlar: always, usually, often, sometimes, never, every (day/week).

Present Continuous (Hozirgi davomiy zamon)

Present Continuous — hozirgi daqiqada yoki yaqin kelajakda davom etayotgan harakatlarni yoki vaqtinchalik vaziyatlarni ifodalaydi.

  • Foydalanish: hozir sodir bo‘layotgan harakatlar, vaqtinchalik vaziyatlar, rejalashtirilgan yaqin kelajak.
  • Tuziq: Subject + to be (am/is/are) + verb-ing.
  • Misollar:
    She is reading a book now. — U hozir kitob o‘qiyapti.
    We are staying in Samarkand this week. — Bu hafta biz Samarqandda turibmiz (vaqtinchalik).
  • Salbiy shakl: subject + to be + not + verb-ing.
    He is not (isn’t) watching TV. — U televizor ko‘rmayapti.
  • So‘roq shakl: To be + subject + verb-ing?
    Are you listening? — Siz eshityapsizmi?

Signal so‘zlar: now, at the moment, right now, today, this week.

To be fe’li (am / is / are)

To be — hozirgi zamonda shaxs va son bo‘yicha quyidagicha qo‘llaniladi:

  • I → am
  • He/She/It → is
  • We/You/They → are

Misollar:

  • I am a student. — Men talabaman.
  • She is happy. — U baxtli.
  • They are at home. — Ular uyda.

Salbiy shakl: am not / is not (isn’t) / are not (aren’t).

So‘roq shakl: Am/Is/Are + subject ...?
